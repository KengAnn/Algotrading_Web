      Recommendation

      The Clenow Momentum approach involves using an exponential regression to measure the direction and quality of price movements of stocks, where direction is based on the annualised coefficient (the slope of of the exponential regression) and quality is based on the R2 of the exponential regression. These two are then combined to establish a ranking system for choosing the highest momentum stocks at any point in time.

      Clenow Momentum = Annualised Slope of Regression Coefficient (%) x R2 of Regression (%)

      Where the rolling period of the exponential regression slope = 90 days.
